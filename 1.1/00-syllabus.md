# Deutsch A1.1 — Kursplan (Syllabus)

**Goethe-Institut Style Course**
**Level:** A1.1 (Beginner — First Half of A1)
**Duration:** 4 weeks, 3 sessions per week (12 sessions total)
**Session Length:** 45 minutes each
**Total Instruction Time:** 9 hours

---

## Course Goals

By the end of this course, the learner will be able to:

- Introduce themselves and others (name, origin, age, occupation)
- Understand and use familiar everyday expressions and very basic phrases
- Ask and answer simple questions about personal details
- Count, tell the time, and talk about prices
- Order food and drink, shop for basics
- Describe their home, family, and daily routine
- Understand short, simple texts (signs, notices, forms)
- Write short personal messages and fill in forms

---

## Grammar Progression

| Week | Grammar Topics |
|------|----------------|
| 1 | Personal pronouns, verb conjugation (sein, heißen, kommen, sprechen), yes/no questions, W-questions, word order |
| 2 | Definite/indefinite articles (Nominative + Accusative), negation (nicht/kein), separable verbs, modal verb "möchten" |
| 3 | Possessive articles, "es gibt" + Accusative, modal verb "können", prepositions (in, auf, an + Dativ), compound nouns |
| 4 | Imperative (du-form), modal verb "müssen", review of all cases and verb forms |

## Lesson Structure (Each 45-Minute Session)

Each lesson follows a consistent format:

1. **Einstieg (Warm-up)** — 5 min
   - Review of previous lesson / small talk in German

2. **Wortschatz (Vocabulary)** — 10 min
   - New vocabulary with images/context
   - Pronunciation practice

3. **Grammatik (Grammar)** — 10 min
   - Grammar explanation with examples
   - Pattern drills

4. **Übungen (Practice)** — 15 min
   - Dialogues and role-plays
   - Listening/reading exercises
   - Fill-in-the-blank and matching tasks

5. **Zusammenfassung & Hausaufgabe (Wrap-up & Homework)** — 5 min
   - Key takeaways
   - Homework assignment for self-study

## Tips for the Tutor

- **Speak German as much as possible** — even at A1.1, immersion helps
- **Use gestures and real objects** — point to things, act out verbs
- **Repeat, repeat, repeat** — say new words at least 5 times in different contexts
- **Correct gently** — recast errors naturally ("Ich kommen..." → "Ah, du kommst aus...")
- **Celebrate small wins** — every new sentence is progress
- **Practice between sessions** — encourage 10–15 min daily vocab review
- **Use German media** — Easy German YouTube, Deutsche Welle "Nicos Weg" (free A1 series)
